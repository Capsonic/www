Product Information

Capsonic designs, manufactures and tests solenoid and coil products. Some solenoid applications will also include additional functional content, such as valves, latching mechanisms, feedback devices, or pre-wired interconnects. We overmold some coil assemblies for protected use in harsh environments. 100% electrical and core mechanical function testing is part of the production process on all of our products, leading to infallible quality provided to our customers

Process Info

  • Automated & Semi-Automated Coil Winding – Lead terminations automated & manual
  • Automated & Semi-Automated Assembly
  • 100% Electrical Testing on every internal line, Force/Displacement Testing, Impedance Testing, Lock/Latch Point Testing
  • Integral Wire Harness and Connections (Weld & IDC Connect), Protection Circuitry, Switch and Sensor Integrated Feedback
  • Solderless Connection Processes – Compliant Pin, Weld, IDC
  • Insert Molding for Coil Overmolding and Bobbins
  • Full Design & Development: Magnetic Analyses, Concept, Prototype, Validation Testing, Tooling, Production Verification, Continued Conformance
