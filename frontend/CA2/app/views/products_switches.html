Product Information

Capsonic designs, tests and manufactures many types of electrical switches, both contact and non-contact, for use in vehicle/aircraft interiors and vehicle exterior/underhood interfaces. Some of our products are developed for hidden use in subsystems to provide control feedback.

Switch Product Technologies

  • Low Current & High Current
    • Contact
    • Non-Contact
    • Capacitive
    • Magnetic
  • Micro-Switch & Tact Switch
    • Contact & Non-Contact
  • Switch Cell technology
  • Class A Decorative Surface Processing
  • Backlighting
  • Ergonomic & Hidden
  • PCB-Based Switches & Contacts

Process Capabilities

  • Laser Etching; Negative Contrast & Etch thru Chrome technologies available via outside partners
  • Pad Printing – Multiple Hit; various Z-Axis placements
  • 100% Electrical Testing of every internal line; Force/Displacement Testing; Make/Break Testing
  • Soldering & PCB Processing – No Clean & Lead-Free Solder; PCB Cleaning; RoHS Migration
  • Solderless Connection Technology
  • Insert Molding
  • Full Design & Development: Concept, Prototype, Validation Testing, Tooling, Production Verification, Continued Conformance
