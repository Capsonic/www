Capsonic Alliances

Capsonic has formed an Alliance with BGM Engineering to broadly expand technical design capabilities to include all aspects of Electronic Design and Integration.

BGM maintains Engineering Design Staff, CAD/CAE systems for Analysis, full laboratory for Product test and assessment, prototype capabilities, and supply tier technology partners for Advance development of new electronic technologies.

Capsonic and BGM will act as one Design and Product Supply entity so to provide SINGLE SUPPLY Solution to customers for ALL Types of Sub-System & Assembly products.
